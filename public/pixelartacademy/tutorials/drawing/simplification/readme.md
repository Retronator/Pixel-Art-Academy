### Images used under fair use

Intentional simplification lesson features sprites from the following games:

- Green Beret, Imagine Software, 1986
- Peter Beardsley's International Football, Grandslam Entertainments, 1988
- Dynamite Dan, Mirrorsoft, 1985
- Skool Daze, Microsphere, 1984
- Sir Fred, Made in Spain, 1986
- Underwurlde, Ultimate Play The Game, 1984
- Rick Dangerous, Firebird Software, 1989
- Head over Heels, Ocean Software, 1987
- Stop the Express, Sinclair Research, 1983
- Atic Atac, Ultimate Play The Game, 1983
- Cookie, Ultimate Play The Game, 1983
- I Ball II, Firebird Software, 1987
- Dizzy, Code Masters, 1987

The artworks are chosen based on their historical and educational relevance. The purpose is for students to
get familiar with early computer graphics from an art history standpoint and study the techniques developed
in those times from an art practice standpoint.

If you are a copyright holder of any of the works and do not wish them to be included in this manner,
write to hi@retronator.com and they will be removed from the curriculum.
